# Lightsabers Reference

Complete reference for all lightsaber variants available in Orbis Galactic, including stats, abilities, and crafting requirements.

## Darksaber

<Card title="Darksaber" icon="sword">
  **Quality:** Legendary  
  **Item Level:** 40  
  **Durability:** 150

  An ancient black-bladed lightsaber with a white-edged core, emitting a low, unstable hum. Heavy and resistant to the untrained hand, it is a powerful symbol of authority and legacy among the Mandalorians.

  ### Stats
  - **Base Damage (Swing):** 48 Physical
  - **Charged Attack (Stab):** 144 Physical
  - **Thrust Damage:** 32 Physical
  - **Random Damage Modifier:** ±15%
  - **Durability Loss per Hit:** 0.52

  ### Abilities
  - **Primary Attack:** Advanced attack combo with multiple swing patterns
  - **Secondary:** Block incoming damage
  - **Ability (E):** Toggle blade on/off
  - **Special:** Signature Energy attacks when fully charged (20 energy gain when active)

  ### Animations
  - Ignite/Retract animations with special effects
  - Custom Darksaber trail and particle effects
  - Menacing stance animations

  ### Sound Effects
  - Unique Darksaber hum
  - Attack, equip, and unequip sounds
  - Charged attack audio
</Card>

## General Lightsaber Mechanics

### Animations
All lightsabers use specialized animation sets:
- **Ignite/Retract:** Smooth blade extension/retraction with character expressions
- **Double-Blade:** Special animations for double-bladed variants
- **Combat:** Fluid attack chains with blade trails
- **Wiggle Weights:** Advanced motion physics for realistic movement

### Sound Effects
Each color variant has unique audio:
- Equip/Unequip sounds
- Idle hum (continuous)
- Attack swings
- Charged attack sounds
- Blade collision effects

### Visual Effects
- Color-specific blade trails
- Particle systems
- Dynamic lighting matching blade color
- Blade animation cycles

### Camera Controls
Specialized camera targeting:
- Pitch control targets Head and Shoulders
- Yaw control targets Belly for smooth rotation
- Custom wiggle physics for natural motion

<Note>
  All lightsabers require a **Laser Weapon Bench** to craft. The bench must be placed and accessible before you can create lightsabers.
</Note>
