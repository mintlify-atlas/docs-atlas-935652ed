# Kyber Crystals Reference

Kyber crystals are rare, Force-attuned crystals that power lightsabers. Each crystal type has unique properties and resonates with different aspects of the Force.

---

        **Rarity:** Uncommon  
        **Force Alignment:** Dark Side (Sith)

        ### Description
        A jagged crimson crystal radiating an unstable pulse. Its hum is harsh and discordant, as if the crystal itself is in pain. The Force within feels twisted and forcibly bound.

        ### Properties
        - **Associated Trait:** Domination and malice
        - **Sith Path:** Corrupted through "bleeding"
        - **Force Connection:** Twisted, forcibly bound
        - **Blade Color:** Unstable crimson

        ### Lore
        Red kyber crystals are not natural - they are created when a dark side user forces their will upon a kyber crystal, causing it to "bleed" and turn red. The crystal's structure becomes jagged and unstable, reflecting the pain of corruption. The harsh, discordant hum is evidence of the crystal's suffering.

        ### Creation Process
        Sith create red crystals through a process called "bleeding":
        1. Take a kyber crystal (often stolen from a Jedi)
        2. Pour dark side energy and hatred into it
        3. Break the crystal's connection to the light
        4. Force it to serve the dark side

        ### Usage
        - Sith lightsaber construction
        - Dark side weapons
        - Required for all red lightsaber variants
      </Accordion>

        4. Instead, it becomes white - forever marked by its redemption

## Mining Kyber Crystals

### Kyber Crystal Ore

Kyber crystals can be found in various ore formations across different terrain types:

<Accordion title="Ore Variants">
  - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Stone:** Found in standard stone formations
  - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Basalt:** Located in volcanic regions
  - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Slate:** Found in mountainous areas
  - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Shale:** Located in sedimentary rock formations
  - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Volcanic:** Rare volcanic deposits
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Each ore block has a chance to drop different colored kyber crystals. Rarer crystal colors (Purple, Orange, White, Golden) have lower drop rates from ore blocks.
</Note>

## Crafting Usage

Kyber crystals are essential components for lightsaber construction:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Acquire Crystal">
    Mine kyber crystal ore or find crystals through exploration
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Hilt">
    Select which lightsaber hilt style you want (Anakin, Luke, or Maul)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Gather Materials">
    Collect required materials:
    - 1x Kyber Crystal (desired color)
    - 1x Lightsaber Hilt
    - 5x Fire Essence (10x for double-bladed)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Craft at Bench">
    Use the Laser Weapon Bench to combine components into a lightsaber
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The color of kyber crystal you choose determines your lightsaber's blade color. Choose wisely - each color reflects a different philosophy and connection to the Force!
</Tip>

## Lore and Philosophy

### The Living Crystal

Kyber crystals are not merely objects - they are alive with the Force. They grow in crystal caves across the galaxy and are attuned to the Force in ways that few other materials are.

### Force Bonding

When a Force-sensitive being finds their kyber crystal, the crystal "calls" to them through the Force. This bond is personal and unique - no two relationships between wielder and crystal are exactly alike.

### Crystal Meditation

Jedi younglings traditionally journey to crystal caves where they meditate and allow the Force to guide them to their crystal. The crystal they find reflects who they are and who they may become.

<Warning>
  Kyber crystals are semi-sentient and can feel pain. The process of bleeding a crystal to create a red Sith blade causes genuine suffering to the crystal, which is why their hum becomes harsh and discordant.
</Warning>
