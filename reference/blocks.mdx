## Crafting Stations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Laser Weapon Bench" icon="hammer">
    **Category:** Benches  
    **Purpose:** Crafting Station

    A workstation to manufacture laser-based weaponry.

    ### Capabilities
    - Craft all lightsaber variants
    - Craft all blaster types
    - Required for all laser weapons

    ### Usage
    Place this bench in your base to access lightsaber and blaster crafting recipes. You cannot create laser weapons without this workstation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Ship Call Beacon" icon="signal">
    **Category:** Special Blocks  
    **Purpose:** Transportation

    Place this to call your personal spaceship.

    ### Requirements
    **IMPORTANT:** A free space of **17x5x5 blocks** in front of the beacon is required!

    ### Usage
    - Place beacon in open area
    - Ensure landing zone is clear
    - Activate to summon your ship
    - Use for fast planetary travel
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

      - **Top Right** - Upper right corner window
      - **Bottom Left** - Lower left corner window
      - **Bottom Right** - Lower right corner window

      - **Bottom Center** - Bottom edge window

    </Tab>

      - **Center** - Full center window
      - **Center Left Right** - Horizontal connector
      - **Center Top Bottom** - Vertical connector
    </Tab>
  </Tabs>

  #### Building Guide
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Start with corners">
      Place corner windows at the edges of your window area
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add edges">
      Connect corners with edge pieces along the perimeter
    </Step>
    <Step title="Fill center">
      Use center pieces and connectors to complete the window
    </Step>
  </Steps>

  <Tip>
    The modular window system allows you to create windows of any size by combining different pieces. Plan your window layout before placing blocks!
  </Tip>

## Kyber Crystal Ore

<Card title="Kyber Ore Blocks" icon="gem">
  Natural ore formations containing kyber crystals.

  ### Ore Types
  <Tabs>
    <Tab title="Standard">
      **Kyber Crystal Ore - Stone**
      
      Most common ore type found in stone formations.
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Basalt">
      **Kyber Crystal Ore - Basalt**
      
      Found in volcanic and basalt regions.
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Slate">
      **Kyber Crystal Ore - Slate**
      
      Located in slate and mountainous areas.
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Shale">
      **Kyber Crystal Ore - Shale**
      
      Found in shale formations and sedimentary rock.
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Volcanic">
      **Kyber Crystal Ore - Volcanic**
      
      Rare volcanic deposits with higher crystal yields.
    </Tab>
  </Tabs>

  ### Mining
  - Mine with pickaxe
  - Random chance for colored crystals
  - Rarer colors have lower drop rates
  - Volcanic ore may have better yields

  <Note>
    Each ore block can drop various kyber crystal colors. The ore type affects spawn location but all can potentially yield any crystal color.
  </Note>
</Card>

## Building Tips

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Imperial Structures">
    ### Creating Authentic Builds
    
    1. **Use panel variants** - Mix smooth and brick patterns
    2. **Plan window layouts** - Use the modular system effectively
    3. **Add floor patterns** - Don't forget interior details
    4. **Include lighting** - Imperial lamps for atmosphere
    5. **Color coordination** - Stick to black, gray, and white palette
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Cantinas & Settlements">
    ### Desert Architecture
    
    1. **Layer pipes** - Add depth with piping details
    2. **Antenna placement** - Rooftops and communication towers
    3. **Interior details** - Use cantina lamps and fixtures
    4. **Mix materials** - Combine with standard blocks for variety
    5. **Weathered look** - Mos Eisley style is lived-in and worn
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Spaceships">
    ### Custom Vessels
    
    1. **Hull foundation** - Start with hull blocks for structure
    2. **Window placement** - Strategic viewports
    3. **Mix window types** - Imperial and generic ship windows
    4. **Interior planning** - Leave room for interior spaces
    5. **Landing space** - Remember the Ship Call Beacon requirements
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  When placing a Ship Call Beacon, ensure you have a clear **17x5x5 block** space in front of it. Your ship cannot land without adequate clearance!
</Warning>

## Block Categories in Game

Blocks are organized in the following categories:
- **OrbisGalactic.Blocks** - General building blocks
- **OrbisGalactic.Deco** - Decorative elements
- **Benches** - Crafting stations
- **Special** - Beacons and unique blocks

Access these through your building menu's category filters for easier navigation.
