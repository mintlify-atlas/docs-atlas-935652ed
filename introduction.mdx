# Welcome to Orbis Galactic

Orbis Galactic is a Star Wars mod for Hytale that transforms your game into an immersive galactic adventure. Explore iconic planets, wield legendary lightsabers, and experience the Force like never before.

<Note>
  Version 1.4.0 - Developed by Cerzix, Viluron, Nelayx, and Holag
</Note>

## Lightsabers

Orbis Galactic features an extensive collection of lightsabers with authentic designs and functionality.

### Hilt styles

Choose from three iconic hilt designs:

- **Anakin hilt** - A finely crafted lightsaber hilt of elegant, functional design. Carries a powerful presence shaped by great promise and tragic destiny
- **Luke hilt** - A refined lightsaber hilt built with careful intention and quiet resolve. Forged by a Jedi who sought to restore hope
- **Maul hilt** - A brutal, double-coupled lightsaber hilt forged for relentless aggression. Available in single, double, and separated configurations

### Kyber crystal colors

Each kyber crystal color represents different Force alignments and philosophies:

| Color | Description | Force Alignment |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **Blue** | Steady and resolute, resonating with discipline and resolve | Light Side - Guardians |
| **Green** | Alive with flowing resonance, echoing patience and wisdom | Light Side - Consulars |
| **Red** | Jagged crimson radiating unstable pulse, the crystal itself in pain | Dark Side - Corrupted |
| **Purple** | Deep violet with conflicting energies, balanced on a razor's edge | Neutral - Balance |
| **Gold** | Radiant golden emitting pure harmony, ancient and unwavering | Light Side - Legendary |
| **Orange** | Warm glow with restrained intensity, shaped by personal will | Neutral - Pragmatic |
| **White** | Rare prismatic crystal, purified and self-determined | Neutral - Redemption |

<Note>
  Use Secondary Ability (E) to extend and retract lightsaber blades
</Note>

### The Darksaber

An ancient black-bladed lightsaber with a white-edged core, emitting a low, unstable hum. Heavy and resistant to the untrained hand, it is a powerful symbol of authority and legacy among the Mandalorians.

## Blasters

Equip yourself with authentic Star Wars weaponry:

- **DC-15A** - The standard issue blaster rifle for clone troopers
- **Cycler Rifle** - A long range high velocity blaster rifle often used by Tusken Raiders

## Planets

### Tatooine

The desert planet features multiple biomes and environments:

- Desert shores and rocky canyons
- Mos Eisley Cantina and city buildings
- Tusken Raider camps
- Jawa Crawler and scrap heaps
- Ship wreckage scattered across the dunes
- Variable weather including sunny, blazing heat, and sandstorms

### Ilum

The ice planet where kyber crystals are found:

- Massive ice spike formations
- Kyber crystal ore nodes in various stone types
- Frozen caves and crystal caverns
- Harsh, cold weather conditions

<Warning>
  Kyber crystals can only be mined on Ilum. You'll need to travel there to obtain these precious Force-attuned gems.
</Warning>

## Kyber crystals

Kyber crystals are rare, Force-attuned crystals essential for lightsaber construction. Each crystal type can be found in different ore variations:

- Kyber Crystal Ore - Stone
- Kyber Crystal Ore - Basalt
- Kyber Crystal Ore - Slate
- Kyber Crystal Ore - Shale
- Kyber Crystal Ore - Volcanic

### Clear kyber crystals

A transparent shimmering crystal emitting a faint humming from its center. These crystals lack Force attunement and must be bonded with a Force user to gain their color and power.

## NPCs and creatures

Encounter iconic Star Wars characters and creatures:

- **Jawas** - Small scavengers who trade scrap metal for valuable items
- **Tusken Raiders** - Desert warriors defending their territory
- **Stormtroopers** - Imperial soldiers patrolling certain areas
- **R2D2 Droids** - Astromech droids available through Jawa traders
- **Twileks and Rodians** - Various alien species inhabiting settlements

## Trading and economy

The galactic credit is the standard currency:

- **Silver Credit** - The galactic standard currency
- **Gold Credit** - Worth 10 silver credits

### Jawa scrap trader

Jawas operate scrap trading shops that refresh daily at 6:00 AM:

- Trade 100 scrap metal for a Speeder Bike (2 in stock)
- Trade 50 scrap metal for an R2D2 Droid Pod (2 in stock)
- Trade 20 scrap metal for Silver Credits (unlimited)

<Note>
  Scrap metal can be found scattered across Tatooine in ship wreckage and scrap heaps
</Note>

## Crafting and workstations

**Laser Weapon Bench** - A workstation to manufacture laser-based weaponry. Use this to craft blasters and other energy weapons.

## Space travel

**Ship Call Beacon** - Place this beacon to call your personal spaceship and travel between planets.

<Warning>
  A free space of 17x5x5 blocks in front of the beacon is required for your ship to land!
</Warning>

**Speeder Bike** - A fast vehicle perfect for planetary travel. Right-click to deploy and ride across desert sands or icy plains.
