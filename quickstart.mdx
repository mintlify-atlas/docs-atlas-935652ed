# Quick start guide

This guide will help you get started with Orbis Galactic, from obtaining your first kyber crystal to wielding a fully functional lightsaber.

## Your first steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new game or load your world">
    Launch Hytale with Orbis Galactic installed and either:
    - Create a new world to explore the full mod experience
    - Load an existing world to add Star Wars content
    
    <Note>
      For the best experience, create a new world to ensure proper planet generation
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Gather initial resources">
    Before traveling to find kyber crystals, you'll need:
    - Basic mining tools (pickaxe)
    - Food and survival supplies
    - Torches for cave exploration
    - A crafted Ship Call Beacon (for space travel)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Travel to Ilum">
    Kyber crystals can only be mined on the ice planet Ilum.
    
    To get there:
    1. Craft a Ship Call Beacon
    2. Place the beacon with at least 17x5x5 blocks of free space in front
    3. Activate the beacon to call your spaceship
    4. Board the ship and navigate to Ilum
    
    <Warning>
      Make sure you have the required free space or your ship won't land!
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mine kyber crystals on Ilum">
    Once on Ilum, look for kyber crystal ore nodes:
    
    - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Stone** (most common)
    - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Basalt**
    - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Slate**
    - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Shale**
    - **Kyber Crystal Ore - Volcanic**
    
    Mine these ores to obtain Clear Kyber Crystals. Each crystal type you find will determine your lightsaber's blade color and Force alignment.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Obtain a lightsaber hilt">
    You'll need a lightsaber hilt to complete your weapon. Choose from:
    
    - **Lightsaber Hilt (Anakin)** - Elegant and functional, shaped by great promise
    - **Lightsaber Hilt (Luke)** - Refined with quiet resolve, built to restore hope
    - **Lightsaber Hilt (Maul)** - Brutal double-coupled design for aggressive combat
    
    <Note>
      In creative mode, you can spawn these directly. In survival, check the Laser Weapon Bench for crafting recipes
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Craft your lightsaber">
    Combine your kyber crystal with the lightsaber hilt at a Laser Weapon Bench:
    
    1. Place a Laser Weapon Bench (craftable workstation)
    2. Add your chosen hilt
    3. Add your kyber crystal (the color determines your blade)
    4. Craft your lightsaber
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using your lightsaber

Once you have your lightsaber, here's how to use it:

### Extending and retracting the blade

<Note>
  Use Secondary Ability (E key) to extend and retract the lightsaber blade
</Note>

1. Equip your lightsaber in your hotbar
2. Press **E** to ignite the blade
3. Press **E** again to retract it

### Combat mechanics

- **Attack** - Use primary attack to swing your lightsaber
- **Block** - Some lightsaber variants support blocking
- **Dual wielding** - Maul separated double lightsabers can be wielded in both hands

### Lightsaber variants

Depending on your hilt choice, you'll have different combat styles:

| Hilt Type | Configuration | Combat Style |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Anakin | Single blade | Standard Jedi combat |
| Luke | Single blade | Defensive and balanced |
| Maul - Single | Single blade | Aggressive strikes |
| Maul - Double | Double-bladed | Sweeping attacks |
| Maul - Separated | Dual wield | Overwhelming offense |

## Understanding kyber crystal colors

The kyber crystal you use determines your blade color and Force alignment:

### Light Side crystals

**Blue Kyber Crystal**
- Steady and resolute, resonating with discipline and resolve
- Preferred by Jedi Guardians and protectors
- Best for defensive combat styles

**Green Kyber Crystal**
- Alive with flowing resonance, echoing patience and wisdom
- Favored by Jedi Consulars who master the Force
- Ideal for Force-focused gameplay

**Golden Kyber Crystal**
- Radiant golden emitting pure harmony
- Ancient and unwavering, exceptionally rare
- Grants exceptional clarity and authority

### Dark Side crystals

**Red Kyber Crystal**
- Jagged crimson radiating unstable pulse
- The crystal itself is in pain, twisted by the dark side
- High damage output with aggressive combat bonus

### Neutral crystals

**Purple Kyber Crystal**
- Deep violet with conflicting energies
- Balanced on a razor's edge between control and aggression
- For those who walk between light and dark

**Orange Kyber Crystal**
- Warm glow with restrained intensity
- Shaped by personal will and unconventional purpose
- Pragmatic balance for independent Force wielders

**White Kyber Crystal**
- Rare prismatic crystal, purified of corruption
- Reflects neutrality, redemption, and independence
- Free from both Jedi and Sith traditions

## Exploring Tatooine

While Ilum is essential for kyber crystals, Tatooine offers rich exploration opportunities:

### Key locations

**Mos Eisley**
- Visit the iconic Cantina
- Trade with NPCs
- Explore city buildings and walls
- Meet various alien species (Twileks, Rodians)

**Tusken Raider camps**
- Beware of hostile Tusken Raiders
- Loot camps for resources and weapons
- Find Cycler Rifles used by Tuskens

**Jawa trading posts**
- Trade scrap metal for valuable items
- Purchase R2D2 Droid Pods (50 scrap metal)
- Buy Speeder Bikes (100 scrap metal)
- Exchange scrap for Silver Credits (20 scrap metal)

<Note>
  Jawa shops refresh daily at 6:00 AM game time with limited stock
</Note>

**Ship wreckage sites**
- Scattered across the desert
- Source of scrap metal
- Various configurations: wings, thrusters, cockpits, random parts
- Also find scrap heaps with valuable materials

### Tatooine survival tips

<Warning>
  Tatooine features harsh weather conditions:
  - Sunny weather (standard)
  - Blazing heat (increased temperature, bring water)
  - Sandstorms (reduced visibility)
</Warning>

## Advanced gameplay

### Acquiring the Darksaber

The legendary Darksaber is a unique weapon:

- Ancient black-bladed lightsaber with white-edged core
- Emits a low, unstable hum
- Heavy and resistant to untrained wielders
- Symbol of authority among Mandalorians
- More challenging to control than standard lightsabers

### Building your arsenal

**DC-15A Blaster Rifle**
- Standard issue clone trooper weapon
- Craft at the Laser Weapon Bench
- Ranged alternative to lightsabers
- Effective against multiple enemies

**Cycler Rifle**
- Long range, high velocity blaster
- Often used by Tusken Raiders
- Excellent for sniping
- Can be looted from Tusken camps

### Planetary travel

**Using the Speeder Bike**
1. Obtain a Speeder Bike (trade with Jawas or craft)
2. Right-click to deploy the bike
3. Mount and ride across planets
4. Perfect for covering large distances quickly

**Ship Call Beacon setup**
```
Required space: 17 blocks (length) x 5 blocks (height) x 5 blocks (width)

[Beacon] → [17 blocks of free space] →
```

Place beacon, ensure clear space, activate, and board your ship.

### Galactic credits

Use credits to:
- Trade with NPCs
- Purchase items from shops
- Barter at Stormtrooper and Jawa trading posts

</Note>

## Common beginner mistakes

### Forgetting to extend the blade

Many new players forget that lightsabers start retracted. Always press **E** to ignite your blade before combat!

### Insufficient ship landing space

The Ship Call Beacon requires exactly 17x5x5 blocks of free space. Mark out this area before placing your beacon.

### Mining kyber crystals on wrong planets

Kyber crystals only spawn on Ilum. Don't waste time searching on Tatooine or other planets.

### Not managing scrap metal

Scrap metal is valuable on Tatooine. Collect it from:
- Ship wreckage (wings, thrusters, cockpits)
- Scrap heaps
- Trading with NPCs

Save it for important Jawa trades like the Speeder Bike.
