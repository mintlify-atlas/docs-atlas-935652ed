Orbis Galactic features an immersive audio experience with authentic Star Wars sound effects and music.

## Lightsaber sounds

Each lightsaber action produces iconic sounds that vary by crystal color.

### Sound categories

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Ignition">
    **Sound event:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Lightsaber_Equip_[Color]_Local`
    
    The distinctive snap-hiss when activating your lightsaber.
    
    **Available colors:** Blue, Green, Red, Gold, Purple, Orange, White
    
    **Example:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Lightsaber_Equip_Blue_Local`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Hum">
    **Sound event:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Lightsaber_Hum_[Color]_Local`
    
    The constant hum while your lightsaber is active.
    
    **Audio properties:**
    - Volume: 10
    - Category: AudioCat_Sword
    - Layer volume adjustment: -15 dB
    - Prevent sound interruption: false
    
    **Example file:** `Sounds/Weapons/Lightsaber/Blue/Lightsaber_Blue_Idle_Local.ogg`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Retraction">
    **Sound event:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Lightsaber_Unequip_[Color]_Local`
    
    The sound when deactivating your lightsaber blade.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Attacks">
    **Sound events:**
    - Normal attack: `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Lightsaber_Attack_[Color]_Local`
    - Charged attack: `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Lightsaber_Charged_Attack_[Color]_Local`
    - Collision: `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Lightsaber_Collision_Local`
    
    Whoosh and crackle sounds during combat.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Darksaber sounds

The Darksaber has unique audio separate from standard lightsabers:

- **Equip:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Darksaber_Equip`
- **Hum:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Darksaber_Hum`
- **Attack:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Darksaber_Attack`
- **Charged attack:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Darksaber_Charged_Attack`
- **Unequip:** `OrbisGalactic_SFX_Darksaber_Unequip`

<Info>
  The Darksaber's unstable blade produces a distinctive, harsher sound compared to traditional lightsaber crystals.
</Info>

## Music

### Cantina Band

**Music event:** `Cantina_Band`

**File location:** `Server/Audio/SoundEvents/Music/Cantina_Band.json`

The iconic Star Wars cantina music featuring two variations:

```json
{
  "Layers": [
    {
      "Files": [
        "Sounds/Music/Star Wars Cantina Band 1.ogg",
        "Sounds/Music/Star Wars Cantina Band 2.ogg"
      ],
      "Looping": true,
      "RoundRobinHistorySize": 1,
      "Volume": 4.28
    }
  ],
  "MaxDistance": 46,
  "MaxInstance": 1,
  "StartAttenuationDistance": 15,
  "AmbientDuckingVolume": -5,
  "MusicDuckingVolume": -30,
  "AudioCategory": "AudioCat_Music"
}
```

#### Music properties

- **Looping:** Yes
- **Max distance:** 46 blocks
- **Attenuation starts:** 15 blocks
- **Max instances:** 1 (prevents overlapping)
- **Round robin:** Alternates between two versions
- **Ambient ducking:** -5 dB (lowers ambient sounds)
- **Music ducking:** -30 dB (lowers other music)

<Tip>
  The cantina music uses ducking to automatically lower other audio when it plays, ensuring you can always hear the band clearly.
</Tip>

## Audio implementation

### Sound events

Sound events are JSON files that define:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Layers">
    Multiple audio files can be layered together:
    
    ```json
    "Layers": [
      {
        "Files": ["sound1.ogg"],
        "Volume": -15
      }
    ]
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Distance">
    **MaxDistance:** How far the sound travels
    
    **StartAttenuationDistance:** When volume begins to decrease
    
    Example: Cantina music can be heard up to 46 blocks away.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Ducking">
    **AmbientDuckingVolume:** How much to lower ambient sounds
    
    **MusicDuckingVolume:** How much to lower other music
    
    Ensures important sounds are always audible.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Instances">
    **MaxInstance:** Maximum simultaneous plays
    
    Prevents audio overlap and performance issues.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Item sounds

**Lightsaber sounds:** `Server/Audio/ItemSounds/OrbisGalactic_Sounds_Lightsaber.json`

Defines which sounds play for lightsaber interactions.

### File formats

All audio files use the `.ogg` format for optimal compression and quality.

**Example paths:**
- `Sounds/Weapons/Lightsaber/Blue/Lightsaber_Blue_Idle_Local.ogg`
- `Sounds/Music/Star Wars Cantina Band 1.ogg`

<Warning>
  Sound events with `"PreventSoundInterruption": false` can be interrupted by other sounds. Critical audio should set this to `true`.
</Warning>

## Customization

You can customize audio by:

1. Adjusting volume levels in sound event JSON files
2. Replacing `.ogg` files with your own recordings
3. Modifying distance and attenuation settings
4. Adding new sound layers to existing events

<Info>
  Each lightsaber color has its own complete sound set, allowing for unique audio experiences based on your crystal choice.
</Info>
