Orbis Galactic adds an extensive arsenal of Star Wars weapons to Hytale, from elegant lightsabers to powerful blasters.

## Lightsabers

Lightsabers are the signature weapons of the Jedi and Sith. You can craft them using kyber crystals and lightsaber hilts at the Laser Weapon Bench.

### Lightsaber styles

The mod features three distinct lightsaber hilt styles:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Anakin" icon="sword">
    A finely crafted lightsaber hilt of elegant, functional design. Once wielded by a legendary Jedi Knight, it carries a powerful presence shaped by great promise and tragic destiny.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Luke" icon="sword">

### Darksaber

**Darksaber** (`OrbisGalactic_Weapon_Lightsaber_Darksaber`)

An ancient black-bladed lightsaber with a white-edged core, emitting a low, unstable hum. Heavy and resistant to the untrained hand, it is a powerful symbol of authority and legacy among the Mandalorians.

### Crafting lightsabers

To craft a lightsaber, you need:

1. A lightsaber hilt (Anakin, Luke, or Maul)
2. A kyber crystal in your desired color
3. 5 Fire Essence
4. Access to a Laser Weapon Bench (Tier 1 or higher)

<Accordion title="Example: Blue Lightsaber (Anakin)">
  **Item ID:** `OrbisGalactic_Weapon_Lightsaber_Anakin_Blue`
  
  **Quality:** Epic
  
  **Recipe:**
  - 1x Lightsaber Hilt (Anakin) (`OrbisGalactic_Lightsaber_Hilt_Anakin`)
  - 1x Blue Kyber Crystal (`OrbisGalactic_Kyber_Crystal_Blue`)
  - 5x Fire Essence (`Ingredient_Fire_Essence`)
  
  **Crafting time:** 4.5 seconds
  
  **Bench required:** Laser Weapon Bench (Lightsabers - Single category)
</Accordion>

## Blasters

Blasters are ranged energy weapons commonly used throughout the galaxy.

### DC-15A

**DC-15A** (`OrbisGalactic_Weapon_DC15A`)

The standard issue blaster rifle for clone troopers.

- **Quality:** Rare
- **Impact damage:** 15
- **Bullet spread:** 0.02
- **Parent template:** `OrbisGalactic_Blaster_Template`
- **Model:** `Items/Weapons/Blaster_DC15A/Blaster_DC15A_Model.blockymodel`

### Cycler Rifle

**Cycler Rifle** (`OrbisGalactic_Weapon_Cycler_Rifle`)

A long range high velocity blaster rifle often used by Tusken Raiders.

<Tip>
  Blasters use projectile-based combat. Aim carefully to hit distant targets!
</Tip>

## Combat mechanics

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Lightsabers">
    - **Primary attack:** Swing the lightsaber with varying attack animations
    - **Secondary ability:** Block incoming damage while holding
    - **Special ability (E):** Extend/retract the blade
    - **Charged attacks:** Hold primary attack for more powerful strikes
    - **Trail effects:** Each color has unique blade trail effects
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Blasters">
    - **Primary attack:** Shoot blaster bolts
    - **Projectile config:** Each blaster uses specific projectile configurations
    - **Impact damage:** Varies by weapon type
    - **Spread:** Affects accuracy at range
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
