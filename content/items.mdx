Orbis Galactic adds various items to enhance your Star Wars experience in Hytale.

## Kyber crystals

Kyber crystals are Force-attuned crystals essential for crafting lightsabers.

### Clear Kyber Crystal

**Clear Kyber Crystal** (`OrbisGalactic_Kyber_Crystal_Clear`)

A transparent shimmering crystal. You can hear a faint humming resonating outwards from its center. It seems to be lacking force.

<Info>
  Clear kyber crystals serve as the base material. You can infuse them with color at an Arcane Bench.
</Info>

### Colored crystals

You can transform clear kyber crystals into colored variants:

<Accordion title="Blue Kyber Crystal recipe">
  **Item ID:** `OrbisGalactic_Kyber_Crystal_Blue`
  
  **Recipe:**
  - 1x Clear Kyber Crystal (`OrbisGalactic_Kyber_Crystal_Clear`)
  - 30x Blue Crystal (`Ingredient_Crystal_Blue`)
  
  **Bench required:** Arcanebench (Arcane_Misc category)
  
  **Quality:** Epic
  
  **Light color:** `#065`
  
  **Particle effect:** Blue gem sparks (`#85ecff`)
</Accordion>

### Mining kyber ore

Kyber crystals are found in ore form across various stone types:

- **Kyber Crystal Ore - Stone** (`Ore_Kyber_Stone`)
- **Kyber Crystal Ore - Basalt** (`Ore_Kyber_Basalt`)
- **Kyber Crystal Ore - Slate** (`Ore_Kyber_Slate`)
- **Kyber Crystal Ore - Shale** (`Ore_Kyber_Shale`)
- **Kyber Crystal Ore - Volcanic** (`Ore_Kyber_Volcanic`)

<Tip>
  Search for kyber ore in caves and underground areas on Ilum for the best yields.
</Tip>

## Crafting stations

### Laser Weapon Bench

**Laser Weapon Bench** (`Bench_Laser_Weapon_Crafter`)

A workstation to manufacture laser-based weaponry.

#### Recipe

- 1x Clear Kyber Crystal (`OrbisGalactic_Kyber_Crystal_Clear`)
- 10x Fire Essence (`Ingredient_Fire_Essence`)
- 10x Rock (any rock type)

**Crafting time:** 4 seconds

**Bench required:** Workbench (Tier 2, Workbench_Crafting category)

#### Crafting categories

The Laser Weapon Bench supports three crafting categories:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Weapon Parts">
    Craft lightsaber hilts and other weapon components.
    
    **Category ID:** `Weapon_Parts`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Lightsabers (Single)">
    Craft single-bladed lightsabers including Anakin and Luke styles.
    
    **Category ID:** `Lightsabers_Single`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Lightsabers (Double)">
    Craft double-bladed lightsabers in the Maul style.
    
    **Category ID:** `Lightsabers_Double`
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

#### Upgrades

You can upgrade your Laser Weapon Bench to Tier 2:

**Upgrade requirements:**
- 25x Thorium Bar (`Ingredient_Bar_Thorium`)
- 25x Cobalt Bar (`Ingredient_Bar_Cobalt`)
- 20x Fire Essence (`Ingredient_Fire_Essence`)
- 5x Clear Kyber Crystal (`OrbisGalactic_Kyber_Crystal_Clear`)

**Upgrade time:** 3 seconds

**Benefit:** 15% crafting time reduction

<Info>
  The Laser Weapon Bench emits a blue light (`#134`) and plays unique sound effects when opening, closing, and completing crafts.
</Info>

## Ship Call Beacon

**Ship Call Beacon** (`Ship_Call_Beacon`)

Place this to call your personal spaceship. 

<Warning>
  IMPORTANT: A free space of 17x5x5 blocks in front of the beacon is required!
</Warning>

### Recipe

- 8x Iron Bar (`Ingredient_Bar_Iron`)
- 5x Void Essence (`Ingredient_Void_Essence`)
- 3x Thorium Bar (`Ingredient_Bar_Thorium`)

**Crafting time:** 5 seconds

**Bench required:** Workbench (Workbench_Crafting category)

**Max stack:** 1

### Usage

Place the beacon and interact with it to summon your spaceship. There's a 10-second cooldown between uses.

**Model:** `Resources/BlockModels/Ship_Call_Beacon.blockymodel`

**Texture:** `Resources/BlockTextures/Ship_Call_Beacon_Texture.png`
